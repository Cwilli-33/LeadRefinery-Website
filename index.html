Every record is strategically placed into priority tier's. Each priority tier tells a story and indicates how you should activate that lead. Build new GTM motions around each of the tiers and watch your brokerage scale. 

P1

Call today.

Fresh triggers and a narrow window. We feel the merchant could be in-market this week. Get them into your pipeline and label them "hot".

This week.

Warm.

Workable, but the story is softer. A dial list when the P1–P2 stack runs thin or you are training a Jr. broker or augmenting a call center. 

P4
